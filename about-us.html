About me

Product Mananger | UX/UI Designer | Problem Solver

Hi, My name is Yan Zhao. I am a product designer wearing many different hats. I am based in China most recently. And I am willing to relocate for any great opportunities.

Most recently, I titled as product manager, but also work as a product designer, and sometimes do some UI design. I see design as a tool to provide solutions.
