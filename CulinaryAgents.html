ABOUT

Culinary Agents is a New York-based company. Its product is a professional networking and job matching website designed for current and aspiring professionals in the food, beverage, and hospitality industry. Its website enables talent to represent themselves professionally, match to and seek out job opportunities, get inspired by virtual mentorships, and connect with resources to help them fuel their careers.














My Role



UX Design UI Design

From 2016~2017, I worked as an in house UX/UI designer at Culinary Agents in New York City for a year. My key responsibilities include designing new functions of the website to assist business growing, marketing collateral support, marketing visual digital support, infographic, small design projects and general website design.
