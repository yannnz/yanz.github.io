Oversea First Aid

China Re Life Oversea Tourists First Aid App

Mobile App

Client: China Re Insurance Company

ABOUT

FirstaAid is an one week project designed and developed to aid China Re’s customers seaking emergency medical assistance while traveling overseas. These two versions of the app bellow build an effecient method to connect the patients and first-aid personnels. Patients can send helping messages quickly, and the first-aid personnels will locate the patients' accuratly and get the basic medical information of the him/her ahead.














My Role



Product Design UX Design UI Design Communication

Problem

China ReLife Insurance company offers it’s customer oversea travel insurance . Their clients (Chinese toursits) have difficulty to find medical assisant when needed due to languange boundary and unfamiliarity with the local facilities. When an emergency medical condition happened, it is always risky for tourists.

Inspiration & Solution

In oder to provide a better customer service, China ReLife developed this Oversea Tourists First Aid App. It includes two versions: the tourist version, and the first-aider version. It provides tourists fast apporach to the medical assistant while traveling in a forign country.


The key feature of this project is inspired by Uber dispatching technology. In this case, a neareast avaliable first aider can be dispatched to the patient through the app.
